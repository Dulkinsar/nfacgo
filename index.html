Logic-Gated Cell Therapy Infrastructure

Making solid tumors programmable targets.

GoCART builds recognition dimer CAR-T systems that only trigger when two antigens co-exist on the same cell, reducing off-tumor risk and opening a path beyond blood cancers.

Why Now

Most tumors still have no safe targeting logic.

Roughly 70% of cancers are out of reach for current targeted therapies. Single-antigen CAR-T designs can activate on healthy tissue with low-level antigen expression, creating a hard safety ceiling.

Current Constraint

Conventional CAR-T is transformative in hematological oncology but constrained in solid tumors where tumor-exclusive antigens are rare.

Failure Mode

Single-input activation can trigger at physiologic antigen levels outside tumor tissue, increasing off-tumor toxicity risk.

Strategic Need

The field needs high sensitivity and strict combinatorial specificity at the same time, not as a trade-off.

Recognition Dimer Platform

True AND-gate logic with clinical-grade sensitivity.

GoCART's architecture requires simultaneous engagement of two tumor antigens before full activation, preserving binding strength while enforcing spatial coincidence.

AND Logic, Not Approximation

Activation is gated by dual-antigen recognition on the same cell, minimizing activity from isolated low-level expression.

Modular Binder Stack

Interchangeable binder modules accelerate extension to new antigen pairs and future disease programs.

Scale-Oriented Design

Platformized logic simplifies translation from discovery to preclinical testing and downstream manufacturing.
