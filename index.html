About

CAR T-cells have been shown to have impacted the market due to its target identification, strong binding and target destruction. Though this technique has shown to have results, there are still LOOPHOLES that need to be taken care of.

Several critical challenges of current CAR T-Cell Therapy:

  • On-Target, Off-Tumor Toxicity
    CAR T-cells may attack healthy tissues expressing the same antigens as tumor cells, raising serious safety concerns.
  • Tumor Antigen Escape
    Tumors can downregulate or mutate target antigens, allowing them to evade immune detection and rendering therapy ineffective over time.
  • High Cost and Complex Manufacturing
    Personalized CAR T-cell production is labor-intensive, expensive, and difficult to scale – making accessibility a major barrier.
  • Loss of Therapeutic Efficacy
    T-cell exhaustion and the suppressive tumor microenvironment often lead to reduced persistence and diminished clinical benefit.

Our Technology

Our platform is built on two major innovations:

  • AND-Gate Logic: Our engineered CAR T-cells require multiple tumor-specific markers to activate, ensuring selective targeting and reduced off-tumor toxicity.
  • Hypoxia Switch: Our cells are activated only in low-oxygen (hypoxic) environments, a hallmark of solid tumors. This ensures an additional layer of safety.

What is DeSci?

DeSci is a movement to democratize and decentralize scientific research using Web3 technologies. It replaces outdated, opaque, and often exclusionary funding models with transparent, community-driven alternatives. Through decentralized platforms, researchers can:

  • Raise funds through tokenized science and DAOs
  • Share data openly without institutional gatekeeping
  • Collaborate across borders with global contributors
  • Retain intellectual property and reward early supporters

How GoCART Uses DeSci

  • Community-Governed Innovation
    Our scientific direction is shaped not just by a closed boardroom — but by an open, passionate community of scientists, investors, and patient advocates. Through our partnership with platforms like Molecule and bio.xyz, we are pioneering a model where the public can contribute to and benefit from life-saving research.
  • Token-Based Funding
    Instead of relying solely on grants or VC funding, we're using DeSci tools to tokenize parts of our research, making investment more accessible and aligned with long-term impact. Our future DAO will allow contributors to have voting power over project direction, trial design, and resource allocation.
  • Transparent Research
    Every step of our pipeline — from in silico modeling to preclinical testing — is documented and accessible on-chain or through open-access platforms. This ensures accountability, accelerates reproducibility, and invites global collaboration.

Why It Matters

Traditional biotech R&D is expensive, centralized, and often moves at a pace misaligned with the urgency of patients' needs. By embracing DeSci, GoCART is:

  • Reducing barriers to entry for innovation
  • Accelerating timelines by streamlining funding and collaboration
  • Creating shared value, not just corporate profit
  • Building trust through transparency and decentralization
